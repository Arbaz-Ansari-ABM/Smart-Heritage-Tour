🛡️ Safety Guide

Essential tips for a safe and enjoyable heritage tour across India

Carry Valid ID

Always carry a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, Passport). Most heritage sites require ID proof for entry.

Stay Hydrated

Carry sufficient water especially in summer. Dehydration is common at open-air monuments in Rajasthan and UP.

Sun Protection

Wear sunscreen, a hat, and light cotton clothes. Most heritage sites have limited shade areas.

Emergency Contacts

Police: 100  |  Ambulance: 108  |  Tourist Helpline: 1800-111-363 (Toll Free)

Photography Rules

Check photography permissions before clicking. Some inner sanctums and military areas strictly prohibit cameras.

Footwear

Remove footwear at temples and mosques. Carry a bag to hold them. Wear comfortable walking shoes.
