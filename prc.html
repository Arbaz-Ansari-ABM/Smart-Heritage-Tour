Agra's Culture and Heritage

Agra's Vibrant Culture

Agra is not only home to the Taj Mahal but also a vibrant cultural hub with deep historical roots. The city’s Mughal heritage is reflected in its art, music, and cuisine. Agra’s bustling markets, like Sadar Bazaar, offer a glimpse into the traditional crafts of leatherwork and marble inlay.

Festivals such as the Taj Mahotsav celebrate Agra’s rich traditions, showcasing folk music, dance, and local delicacies. The city’s culinary delights, including the famous petha and Mughlai dishes, attract food enthusiasts from around the globe.
